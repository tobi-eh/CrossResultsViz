Thanks for stopping by!

This is a little project that was motivated by a few questions I was thinking about while "waiting" to get lapped during some recent races:
  • How many riders do I pass or switch positions with over the course of a race?
  • How much slower am I than those riders that are about to lap me?
  • How far along the course am I when the podium finishers cross the finish line?
  • How much would better starts (faster first laps) help me in getting better results?
  • Will gaps between groups of riders increase/decrease over the course of a race?
  • Can I visualize a race's trajectory from lap times alone? What would that look like?
Usually I find the answers to all of life's hard questions on CrossResults.com, but unfortunately it wasn't able to help me with these.
This application was a quick attempt to fix this. I realize there are some issues with this overall approach (e.g. starting positions are whacky (based on CrossResults' Carried Points), a lot happens during a lap that is not captured here, etc) and there are probably some bugs, but I hope you enjoy this tool anyway! Have fun exploring!
Big thanks to CrossResults.com for offering a platform that enables projects like this!
Please Note: This application works best on large screens as the size of the plots will depend on the size of the available screen area. If you are using a smartphone, I suggest you use this application in wide-screen mode.
